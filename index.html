Our Story

Baked with
Love & Passion

Hi, I'm Priya — a passionate home baker from Hyderabad with a dream to share joy through every bite. What started as baking cakes for family and friends in my little kitchen has blossomed into Sweet Crumbs Home Bakery.

Every order is crafted fresh to order — no preservatives, no shortcuts. I use only the finest ingredients: pure butter, farm eggs, Belgian chocolate, and natural flavours. Hygiene and quality are at the heart of everything I do.
